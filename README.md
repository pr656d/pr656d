### 📖 About Me

I am a **Lead Software Engineer** specializing in native Android development, system architecture, and DevOps. I'm an explorer and tinkerer who believes in automating the boring stuff so humans can focus on solving real-world problems.

* 🌐 **Personal Space**: I write, think, and share updates on my dot-grid notebook site at [premp.in](https://premp.in) (check out my [/now page](https://premp.in/now) to see what I'm currently hacking on).
* 🤖 **AI Orchestration**: I build and maintain custom agentic control planes (`tmux` + `git worktrees` + multi-model routing wrappers) to run parallel AI-assisted developer threads.
* 💻 **Disposable by Design**: I treat my hardware as completely ephemeral. I’m a firm believer in "disposable hardware"—if my machine crashes today, I can rebuild my entire developer workflow, tools, and custom configs right away on a brand-new machine within minutes.
* 👨‍👩‍👧 **Off-screen**: I'm dad to a wonderful girl, [Nyomi](https://nyomipatel.com). When I'm not coding or hanging out with her, you can find me wrenching on or riding my Royal Enfield Continental GT 650.
